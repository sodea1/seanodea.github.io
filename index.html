About Me

I’m a full stack software engineer with a professional background in selling enterprise software services at SAP, managing a national, high-growth retail business at CPG startup DripDrop, and helping people overcome their health obstacles as a physical therapy technician.

At SAP, I envied those with technical expertise and felt energized by the interactions I had that enhanced my own technical understanding. In my first week at DripDrop, I learned everything I could about the science of dehydration from DripDrop’s founder/doctor. As a physical therapist technician, patient, and student, I relentlessly pursued knowledge related to the field and questioned existing treatment strategies. In each of my experiences, a common theme emerges: I crave knowledge and understanding of how things work. Coupled with my strong preference for logical reasoning, problem solving, and critical thinking, I decided to pursue a career as a software engineer. The projects I’ve worked on below are a product of my learning thus far after recently completing App Academy’s 16-week intensive full stack bootcamp. I’m excited to continue learning, improving, and testing my abilities.

On a more personal level, I love a good adventure, a nice cold swim in the ocean (San Francisco Bay especially, where I grew up), and a trip somewhere new. The photo of me above is from my trek to Everest Base Camp in Nepal...before I started to look like a zombie. Unfortunately, I never actually made it there, but that's a story for another day.
